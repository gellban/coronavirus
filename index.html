Abstract

The novel coronavirus named COVID-19 starting in China became a pandemic disease in the world approaching approximately 2,966,488 cases by April 26. Social media like Twitter is one of sources to extract people's views. There are different views in tweets, while this study focuses on 3 class labels: awareness, myth, news. In this paper, we compared 3 classifiers to predict the topics of tweets. Two of them are traditional classification algorithms. We also implemented a deep learning text classification using Embedding, CNN and Dense layers with ReLU and Softmax activation functions. In our experiments, deep learning approach gave outperforms the others with 92% accuracy and F1-score.
